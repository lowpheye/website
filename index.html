tue 14 nov 24 - webrings

with a finished project, and with every single piece of infrastructure that i could've possibly conceived ever done, i set off to make my website discoverable to more likeminded people : and what better way to do that than a webring!

a webring is a list of websites that link to the website before themselves in the list and the website after, usually in their main page, and then the last website on the list points to the first, effectively forming a ring. webrings are like social media if social media was cool , like really cool.

facebook bastardized this concept with the "people you might like" tab, and that leads me to think that a procedurally generated "webring" could be a very interesting project, think of how cool it would be to link together the ease-of-use of modern social media with the awesomeness of the hobbyist website community, i know it kind of ruins the concept but it could be a very interesting idea★
